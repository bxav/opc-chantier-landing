# BrickNote - Executive Summary

## Vision

BrickNote is a native iOS application designed to revolutionize construction site management for French BTP (Bâtiment et Travaux Publics) professionals. The app centralizes notes, annotated photos, meeting recordings, and AI-powered assistance in a single, offline-capable tool.

---

## Target Market

### Primary Personas

| Role | Description | Key Needs |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **OPC** | Ordonnancement, Pilotage, Coordination | Meeting transcription, multi-project management, automated reports |
| **Conducteur de travaux** | Site supervisor | Photo documentation, progress tracking, stakeholder communication |
| **Chef de chantier** | Site foreman | Quick capture, offline mode, simple interface for field use |

### Market Size
- 1.5M+ construction professionals in France
- Growing demand for digital transformation in BTP sector
- Limited competition in French-language, offline-first solutions

---

## Product Features

### Core Functionality
1. **Audio Recording & Transcription** - AI-powered meeting transcription with automatic summary generation
2. **Annotated Photos** - Capture and markup images directly on-site
3. **AI Assistant** - Claude-powered assistant with full project context
4. **Multi-Project Management** - Switch between sites seamlessly
5. **Offline Mode** - Full functionality without internet connection
6. **Chronological Journal** - All notes organized by date

### Differentiators
- **100% Offline** - Works in areas with poor connectivity
- **Local Data Storage** - Privacy-first, no cloud dependency
- **French-First** - UI and AI optimized for French construction terminology
- **No Registration** - Zero friction onboarding
